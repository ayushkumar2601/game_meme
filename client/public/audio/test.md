## Key Features
- **No Overlap**: Sounds never play simultaneously - new sounds stop current audio
- **True Randomness**: All 10 files can be selected for fight events (not just a subset)
- **Variety Algorithm**: Prevents consecutive repeats for better audio experience
- **4-Second Limit**: Every sound stops after exactly 4 seconds
- **Character Selection Trigger**: CID sound plays when you reach character selection
